# Simple Camera

## App Overview

The ultimate camera app for those who want to skip the complex settings and just start shooting. With an intuitive interface, this all-in-one tool handles everything from photography and video recording to QR code scanning.

## Key Features

* **Effortless Photography:** Capture precious moments instantly with a single tap of the shutter button.
* **Smooth Video Recording:** Enjoy hassle-free filming with simple start/stop controls.
* **Built-in QR Code Scanner:** No need for a separate app—scan QR codes directly within the dedicated mode.
* **Quick Camera Toggle:** Switch between the front-facing (selfie) and rear cameras with one tap.
* **Timestamp Overlay:** The current date and time are automatically imprinted on the bottom-right of the image. This is perfect for maintaining records or evidence for professional on-site work.

## Simple Operation

* **Zoom & Pan:** Use two fingers to zoom in/out or move the display position.
* **Reset Zoom:** Double-tap the screen to instantly reset the zoom magnification.

### Control Guide (Bottom Buttons)

1. **QR Code Button:** Activates the scanning feature.
2. **Toggle Camera Button:** Switches between the front and rear cameras.
3. **Photo Button:** Captures a still image.
4. **Record Button:** Starts and stops video recording.
