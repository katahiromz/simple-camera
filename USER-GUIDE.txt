# Simple Camera: User Guide

## **App Overview**

This is the ultimate minimalist camera app designed for those who want to "just take a photo" without dealing with complex settings. With its intuitive interface, you can handle photography, video recording, and QR code scanning all in one place.

## **Key Features**

* **Easy Photography**: Capture precious moments instantly just by pressing the shutter button.
* **Smooth Video Recording**: Enjoy effortless filming with a simple tap to start and stop.
* **QR Code Scanner**: Includes a dedicated scanning mode, eliminating the need for a separate reader app.
* **Camera Toggle**: Quickly switch between the front (selfie) and rear (landscape) cameras with a single tap.

## **Simple Operation**

The app is operated using only four buttons located at the bottom of the screen:

* **QR Code Button**: Activates the scanning function.
* **Camera Toggle Button**: Switches between the front and rear cameras.
* **Shutter Button**: Used to take still photos.
* **Record Button**: Starts and stops video recording.

> **Note**: You can check the app's version information at any time by tapping the **gear icon** in the top right corner.
